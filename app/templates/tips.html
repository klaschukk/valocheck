Fundamentals

Crosshair Placement

Always keep your crosshair at head level. Pre-aim common angles before peeking. This single habit will improve your K/D by 30-50%. Use the crosshair generator to find the perfect reticle.

Essential All Ranks
Economy

When to Eco

Full save on pistol loss round 2. Force buy round 3 if you won pistol. Never half-buy on round 4 -- commit to full save or full buy. Coordinate with team.

Beginner Iron - Silver
Aim

Counter-Strafing

Tap the opposite movement key before shooting. In Valorant, your first bullet is accurate almost instantly after counter-strafing. Practice in the Range with the Vandal.

Intermediate Gold - Platinum
Positioning

Off-Angles

Don't hold the most obvious angle. Step 1-2 meters off the expected position. Enemies will pre-aim the common spot and you'll get a free kill. Change positions after each kill.

Intermediate Gold - Diamond
Agents

Agent Selection for Climbing

Master 2-3 agents, not all of them. For solo queue: Reyna/Jett (duelist), Omen/Astra (controller), Sova/Fade (initiator), Cypher/Killjoy (sentinel). Pick what fills team comp gaps.

Beginner All Ranks
Game Sense

Reading the Minimap

Check minimap every 3-5 seconds. If you see 3 enemies on one site, rotate early. Call out numbers. "2 on A, 1 mid" gives your team information advantage even without voice comms.

Intermediate Silver - Platinum
Advanced

Jiggle Peeking

Quick-peek an angle to bait a shot, then re-peek when the enemy is resetting. Use A/D strafe to expose minimal body. Essential against Operators and holding AWPers.

Advanced Diamond+
Mentality

Dealing with Tilt

After 2 consecutive losses, take a 10-minute break. Drink water. Don't blame teammates in chat -- it only makes everyone play worse. Focus on YOUR mistakes, not others'.

Essential All Ranks
Advanced

Trading Kills

When your teammate peeks and dies, you MUST refrag within 1-2 seconds. Shadow your duelist 3-5 meters behind. Trading ensures your team never goes down in numbers.
