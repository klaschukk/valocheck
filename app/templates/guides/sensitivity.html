Sensitivity Guide

How to find your perfect sensitivity in Valorant. Pro settings, eDPI, and tuning tips.

What is eDPI?

eDPI = DPI x In-Game Sensitivity

This is the only number that matters. Two players with different DPI but the same eDPI will have identical mouse movement in-game.

How to Find YOUR Sensitivity

  1. Start at 800 DPI, 0.3 in-game sens (eDPI 240) — this is the pro average
  2. Play 3-5 deathmatches. If you're constantly overshooting targets, lower it. If you can't turn fast enough, raise it.
  3. Adjust by 0.02-0.05 at a time. Small changes.
  4. Once comfortable, STOP changing it. Let muscle memory develop for at least 1 week.
  5. Make sure you have enough mousepad space — you need at least 30cm for a full 180 turn at low sens.
