Vandal vs Phantom — Which to Buy?

Choose Vandal if: you hit headshots consistently, play long-range angles, or need one-tap potential at all distances.

Choose Phantom if: you prefer spraying, play close-mid range, or want the advantage of no tracers (enemies can't see where you're shooting from through smokes).

Pro preference: roughly 55% Vandal / 45% Phantom in pro play. It's personal preference — both are equally viable.

Pistol Round Strategy

  • Ghost (500) — best overall pistol buy. One-tap light shield at close range.
  • Frenzy (450) — good for aggressive rushes. Full auto, but inaccurate at range.
  • Classic (free) + abilities — viable if your agent needs expensive utility round 1.
  • Light shield (400) + Classic — survivability over firepower. Decent option.
