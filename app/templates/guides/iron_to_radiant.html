Iron to Radiant Roadmap

What to focus on at every rank to keep climbing. A step-by-step guide through the entire ranked ladder.

Iron → Bronze
~2-4 weeks

Focus: Learn the Game

  • Learn all agent abilities for your 2 mains
  • Learn callout names for 3 maps
  • Play 3-5 deathmatches daily for aim
  • Always buy with your team (don't solo buy)
  • Stop running and shooting — stand still to shoot

What gets you stuck: running while shooting, not using abilities, playing too many agents.

Bronze → Silver
~2-4 weeks

Focus: Crosshair Placement

  • Keep crosshair at head level AT ALL TIMES
  • Pre-aim common angles before peeking
  • Learn counter-strafing (tap opposite key before shooting)
  • Check your minimap every 5 seconds
  • Stop crouching — it makes your head easier to hit

What gets you stuck: aiming at the ground, wide-swinging everything, never checking flanks.

Silver → Gold
~3-6 weeks

Focus: Economy & Utility

  • Master economy: know when to save, force, full buy
  • Use utility BEFORE peeking (flash, smoke, then peek)
  • Learn 3-5 lineups for your main agent
  • Play for trades: shadow your entry fragger
  • Start watching your own VODs after losses

What gets you stuck: dry peeking everything, bad economy, no utility usage.

Gold → Platinum
~1-2 months

Focus: Game Sense

  • Read the minimap constantly — predict enemy rotations
  • Default setups: learn where to play in the first 30 seconds of each round
  • Play off-angles instead of obvious positions
  • Learn to play retake: don't over-rotate
  • Communicate: call out numbers, positions, utility used

What gets you stuck: tunnel vision, over-rotating, ego peeking, not adapting to enemy patterns.

Platinum → Diamond
~2-3 months

Focus: Consistency

  • Aim train 15-20 min daily (Aim Lab / Range)
  • Master 2 agents per role so you can always fill
  • Learn team compositions — what works and what doesn't
  • Start anti-stratting: adapt your play based on enemy patterns
  • Reduce your death count — every death matters more here

What gets you stuck: inconsistency, tilting after losses, one-tricking an agent, poor communication.

Diamond → Ascendant
~3-6 months

Focus: Mastery & Teamplay

  • Play with a consistent duo/trio — communication matters more than ever
  • Study pro matches: watch how they execute sites and play retakes
  • Master your utility timing and coordination
  • Work on mental resilience — Diamond lobbies are tilting
  • Focus on YOUR performance, not teammates
Ascendant → Immortal → Radiant
~6-12+ months

Focus: Everything at Elite Level

  • At this point, raw aim and game sense must both be near-perfect
  • Play in organized teams or scrims
  • Study your opponents (same players in lobbies at this rank)
  • Mechanical mastery: jiggle peeking, spray transfer, movement tricks
  • Mental fortitude: play through bad streaks without tilting
